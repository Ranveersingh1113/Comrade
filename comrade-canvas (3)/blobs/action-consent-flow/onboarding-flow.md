# Onboarding flow

## Leader flow

**Step 1 — Create the room**
Leader creates the room and names the project.

**Step 2 — Leader designation**
Leader is told what the role means: runs onboarding, drives initial task delegation, can update tasks directly. All other decisions require team agreement.

**Step 3 — Context upload**
Leader uploads any combination of:

- Documents (briefs, reports, notes)
- WhatsApp export (.txt file)
- GitHub repository link
- Free-text description of the project

Multiple sources can be combined. At least one is required.

**Step 4 — AI context review**
AI ingests all uploaded context and presents a structured summary:

- Project description
- Key goals and deliverables
- Deadlines detected (if any)
- Team members mentioned (if any)
- Key documents and resources identified

Leader reviews the summary. They can:

- Edit fields directly
- Type corrections or additions in a chat input to the AI
- Ask the AI to re-interpret specific parts

When the leader confirms, context is finalised. This becomes the AI's project memory.

**Step 5 — Invite members**
Leader invites members via link or email. Room is not active until at least one member joins.

**Step 6 — Task delegation (triggered after invites are sent)**
Once invites are sent, the AI prompts the leader to begin task delegation. Two paths:

- *Leader describes intent:* AI structures it into a detailed task list using project context. Leader reviews, edits, and assigns each task to a member.
- *Leader asks AI to decide:* AI generates a full task breakdown from project context. Leader reviews, edits, and assigns.

Tasks are visible immediately but not live until each member confirms. Members confirm when they join the room — they see their proposed tasks as part of the member onboarding flow.

## Member flow

**Step 1 — Join via invite**
Member joins via link or email invite.

**Step 2 — Platform orientation**
Brief, skippable explanation of how the platform works:

- The AI is a silent team member — it reads everything but only speaks when called or when something needs flagging
- You have a private thread with the AI that no one else can see
- Shared docs are tracked — if you haven't opened one after 48 hours you'll get a private nudge
- Contribution is visible to the team as task completion — not a feed of your activity

**Step 3 — Task confirmation**
Member sees their proposed tasks. They can accept, push back with a comment, or propose a change. No task goes live until confirmed.

**Step 4 — Enter the room**
Member enters the group room. Private AI thread is available immediately.
